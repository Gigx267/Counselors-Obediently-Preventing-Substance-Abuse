Social Media Strategic Plan

Counselors Obediently Preventing Substance Abuse

Restoring Lives, Families, and the KC Community through Faith-Based Recovery

Our strategic social media plan focuses on building a targeted Kansas City audience through authentic, engaging content. We will leverage digital marketing to drive referrals, increase program awareness, and connect individuals and families with our recovery services. By establishing trust through compassionate messaging and community engagement, we aim to position COPS as the faith-based recovery solution for the KC region.

1. The Mission Context

Our Mission: To serve the Kansas City community.

We aim to amplify our message and impact.

  • Sobriety is a transformative state of mind.
  • Transformation is achievable through faith in Christ.
  • Enhancing awareness of our programs and services.
  • Organizing awareness events to foster donations and attract investors.

2. Our Psychological Solution:

  • Targeted Empathy & Authenticity: Acknowledge pain without judgment, creating a safe entry point for engagement
  • Lived Experience Focus: Highlight transformative recovery stories from program participants
  • Hope Through Faith: Offer spiritual purpose and strength as pathways to sustainable recovery
  • Community Referral Program: Encourage families and community members to advocate for COPS services
  • Emotional Resonance in Context: Deliver strategically crafted messaging that connects emotionally while maintaining professionalism
3. March–April: Onboarding & Awareness Phase

Objective:
Establish a strong, local-only presence on social media (primarily Facebook + Instagram) to build awareness and audience in Kansas City — before promoting programs or services. Focus on entertaining, relatable content that draws attention gently, reduces stigma, and positions COPS as a caring, community-rooted voice.

Key Actions (March–April Timeline):

  • Account Setup & Optimization:
    Create/claim professional profiles (@COPSRecoveryKC or similar). Use consistent branding: faith-inspired yet modern visuals, bio highlighting "Kansas City faith-based recovery support since 2002", location tags, and contact links.
  • Geo-Targeted Following Strategy:
    Follow only Kansas City-area accounts: local churches, faith communities, family-oriented groups, KC businesses, schools, neighborhood pages, recovery allies (e.g., similar orgs like Healing House KC, First Call, KC Recovery Coalition), pastors, community leaders, and engaged local families. Goal: Build reciprocal follows and organic reach within ~50-mile KC radius.
  • Content Structure – Entertaining & Attention-Grabbing:
    Post 3–5 times/week with light, engaging formats to spark interest and grow followers:
    • Inspirational KC-themed quotes/graphics (e.g., "Hope rises over the KC skyline")
    • Short fun reels/stories: local landmarks with subtle hope messages
    • "Did You Know?" facts on addiction myths (gentle, non-triggering)
    • User polls/questions: "What's one small win you're celebrating today?"
    • Behind-the-scenes: team coffee chats, community glimpses (no heavy service push yet)
    • Entertaining: light humor, motivational memes, or KC pride content tied to resilience & renewal
4. May–August : Engagement & Trust Phase
  • Education Focus:
    Share helpful, gentle content:
    • Hope signs series with recovery facts
    • Faith messages tied to healing
    • Polls asking what helps during tough times
    • Share local mental health events and partners
  • Community Interaction:
    Build two-way engagement:
    • Repost recovery wins from KC residents
    • Live Q&A on hope and hard times
    • Summer renewal posts celebrating KC
    • Spotlight local partner organizations
  • Hope & Soft Introduction:
    Gently introduce COPS while staying positive:
    • Short restoration stories (with consent)
    • More faith quotes on strength and healing
    • Ask followers to tag friends needing hope
    • Behind-the-scenes home and group clips
4. September–December: Conversion & Growth Phase

Objective:
With a trusted Kansas City audience built, transition to heartfelt, solution-focused content that directly addresses the darkness of addiction — for individuals struggling, families in crisis, and friends watching loved ones suffer.

Key Focus & Tactics (September–December):

  • Direct, Compassionate Calls-to-Action:
    Every post includes gentle urgency:
    • "If today feels too heavy, you're not alone. Message us for a confidential chat."
    • "Ready for the first step? Call or tap the link for a no-pressure assessment."
    • Pin a "Get Help Now" post with phone/link/bio link to intake form
    • Use platform tools: Link in bio, FB/IG "Contact" buttons, Stories swipe-ups
  • Targeted Engagement & Nurturing:
    • Respond personally to every DM/comment within hours — build one-on-one trust
    • Run soft geo-targeted boosts/ads (KC only) on high-engagement posts
    • Encourage shares: "Tag someone who needs this hope"
    • Track conversions: Monitor link clicks, messages, calls — refine messaging for Pillars (families/churches) & Seekers (younger individuals)

Expected Outcomes by Year-End:
Increased direct inquiries → higher intake/enrollments via social → measurable growth in program admissions. Audience feels seen, valued, and equipped to take the next step toward Christ-centered healing with COPS.

Conclusion

As we move forward, our commitment to the Kansas City community remains steadfast. Through strategic engagement and compassionate outreach, we aim to foster hope and healing for those affected by addiction. Together, we can create a supportive environment that encourages recovery and transformation.
